# Platform Engineer — Container Platform & Developer Experience

- **Period:** 06/2022 – 02/2024
- **Role:** Platform Engineer, Tech Lead
- **Client:** ShopStream Ltd
- **Industry:** E-Commerce / Retail
- **Location:** United Kingdom (UK)
- **Type:** contract

## Description

Built and operated the internal container platform for a mid-size e-commerce company running 40+ microservices. Owned the full developer experience from local development through production deployment, including CI/CD, observability, and incident management.

## Responsibilities

- Designed and operated EKS clusters across 3 environments (dev, staging, production)
- Built internal developer platform with self-service namespace provisioning
- Implemented GitOps workflow with ArgoCD for all deployments
- Created shared Helm chart library for standardised microservice deployments
- Built monitoring stack (Prometheus, Grafana, PagerDuty integration)
- On-call rotation lead — defined incident response procedures and runbooks
- Mentored 3 junior DevOps engineers
- Led weekly architecture review meetings with development teams

## Key Achievements

- Reduced mean time to recovery (MTTR) from 45 minutes to 12 minutes through improved observability and runbooks
- Cut developer onboarding time from 2 weeks to 2 days with self-service platform tooling
- Achieved 99.95% platform uptime over 18 months
- Standardised deployments across all teams — eliminated 6 different deployment approaches
